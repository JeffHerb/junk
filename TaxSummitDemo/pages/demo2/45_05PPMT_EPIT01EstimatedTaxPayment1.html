Instructions

Quarter

Select the quarter you would like to file from the drop-down list. See estimated tax payment dates.

MCTMT

Beginning in tax year 2015, if you owe any Metropolitan Commuter Transportation Mobility Tax (MCTMT) as a self-employed individual or a partner, you must pay your estimated MCTMT payment with your estimated income tax payment. For more information, see Metropolitan commuter transportation mobility tax.

Payment amount

Your total payment amount is the sum of your New York State, City of New York, City of Yonkers, and MCTMT liabilities. MCTMT liability does not apply to fiduciaries filing Form IT-2106, Estimated Income Tax Payment Voucher for Fiduciaries.
