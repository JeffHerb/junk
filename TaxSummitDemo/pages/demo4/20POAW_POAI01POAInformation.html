Important information before you begin

To successfully complete and submit your power of attorney using this online service, you must:

  • Fill in the required information.
  • Print the POA.
  • Sign and date it.
  • Scan the POA into a single file.
  • Attach the file from your computer.
  • Submit your signed POA.

If you are unable to print, scan and submit the POA now, you will be able to save your progress and return to the POA web application at a later time.

Instructions

Will this POA include you and your spouse?

If yes, both you and your spouse must sign and date the POA.

Spouse Social Security number

Enter your spouse's nine-digit Social Security number (SSN). If they do not have an SSN, enter their New York State tax identification number beginning with "TF."

Do you want this POA to revoke all POAs you have on file for the tax matters granted by this POA?

This POA will remain active until you (the taxpayer) revoke it or your representative withdraws from representing you. For information on how to revoke a POA, or how a representative can withdraw, see Power of Attorney.

This POA is for:

If you select OIC, this POA will apply to all tax types and all tax periods for your offer in compromise case. You must complete a separate POA for all other matters.

Sign tax returns on my behalf

Tax returns include refund/credit applications.

Delegate authority to another individual

If you select this, these representatives do not need the consent of any other representative to make a delegation, unless you add a limitation on authority on the next page.
