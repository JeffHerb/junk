Sales & use tax

For information on how to determine the amount of sales or use tax you owe, see instructions. If you do not owe any sales or use tax, you must enter 0 in this field. Do not leave this field blank.

Total payment amount

Total payment amount is the amount you would expect to pay if you filed your return today. The total payment amount includes the total of any New York State, New York City, Yonkers and MCTMT tax liabilities, plus sales and use tax, minus any prepayments. Prepayments include estimated tax payments, credit carry forwards, credits, and withholding tax payments.
