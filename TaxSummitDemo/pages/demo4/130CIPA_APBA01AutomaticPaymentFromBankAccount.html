Automatic Payment from Bank Account

Your payment will be processed as a recurring monthly direct debit (ACH debit) from your bank account. Provide your bank information below. You will have an opportunity to review and verify your transaction information. The funds must be available on your payment day. However, it may take several days for the payment to post to your bank account. Carefully enter your bank account and routing number information. You risk defaulting on your installment payment agreement if you enter incorrect information that prevents your payments from processing.

Accounts outside the United States.

Banking rules prohibit us from accepting electronic payments from accounts outside the United States (even if the bank has branches or offices within the U.S.).

If the account holder does not have a source of funds from within the United States, payment must be made by check or money order.

Instructions

Bank routing number

Enter your bank's routing number as it appears on your check. The routing number is the nine-digit number that identifies your bank and always starts with 0, 1, 2, or 3. Don't enter the account number or check number as part of the routing number. If you have any questions about this number, please contact your bank or financial institution.

Bank account number

Enter your account number as it appears on your check. The account number is generally to the right of the routing number. Don't enter the routing number or check number as part of the account number. If you have any questions about this number, please contact your bank or financial institution.

Account type

Select the account type from which you want the funds withdrawn.

Account holder (John Doe or XYZ Corp.)

Enter the name(s) of the account holder(s) from which the amount will be withdrawn. For example, enter John and Mary Doe if that is the name on the account.

Monthly Payment day

Any changes you make to your monthly payment day after the 2nd of the month will not be effective until the following month.
