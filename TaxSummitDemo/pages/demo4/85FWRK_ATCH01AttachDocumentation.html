Important reminders before you attach your POA:

  • The POA form must be signed and dated.
  • We will ignore any handwritten changes on the POA form, with the exception of the taxpayer name, signature and date.
  • When you scan, your file name may not contain spaces and must have a doc, docx, tif, tiff, jpg, jpeg, png, or a pdf extension.

Instructions:

  1. Scan the signed and dated POA form with no handwritten changes into a single file.
  2. Browse to find the file.
  3. Select Attach to upload your file into the web application.
  4. When complete, select Continue.
