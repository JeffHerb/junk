Instructions

Social Security number (SSN)

Enter your nine-digit Social Security number (SSN). If you do not have a SSN, Contact us.

Taxpayer year for validation

Select the tax year for the return you will use to verify your identification. We will ask you for specific information from that return.

Filing status

Select your income tax filing status for the year you selected.

Spouse Social Security number

Enter your spouse’s nine-digit Social Security number (SSN). If they do not have a SSN, Contact us.

E-mail address

Enter an email address where we can send your confirmation email. If you do not enter an email address, you will not receive a confirmation email and must print the Transaction Confirmation page when you submit your payment.

Assessment ID

Your assessment ID begins with the letter “L” and is found on your consolidated statement of tax liabilities. Enter your assessment ID in the following format: L-123456789.

Audit case ID

Your audit case ID begins with the letter “X” and is found on your statement of proposed audit change. Enter your audit case ID in the following format: X123456789.

Collection case ID

Your collection case ID begins with the letter “E” and is found on your collection notice. Enter your collection case ID in the following format: E-123456789.

Income execution ID

Your income execution (IE) ID begins with the letter “E” and is found on your Income Execution Billing Notice. Enter your IE ID in the following format: E 111111111-E222-3.

Installment payment agreement ID

Your IPA ID begins with the letter “E” and is found on your Installment Payment Agreement Billing Notice. Enter your IPA ID in the following format: E 111111111-D222-3.

Payment amount

Enter the payment amount as a positive number in dollar and cents format.

Your amount due is shown on your bill. If you do not have your bill, you may login to your Online Services account to view your current balance. If you need additional assistance, please see Help.
