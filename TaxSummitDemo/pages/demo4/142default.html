Applying for the Enhanced STAR exemption?

You must now enroll in the Income Verification Program (IVP) to apply or reapply for the Enhanced STAR exemption. Don't worry—you only need to enroll once, and you’ll receive the exemption each year you're eligible!

Note: If you're a new homeowner or first-time STAR applicant, you need to register for the STAR credit.
