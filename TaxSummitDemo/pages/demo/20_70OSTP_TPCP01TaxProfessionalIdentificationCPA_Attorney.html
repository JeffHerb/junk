Instructions

Certified public accountant (CPA)license number

Enter your six-digit certified public accountant (CPA) license number issued by the New York State Education Department.

New York State attorney registration number

Enter your seven-digit attorney registration number issued by the New York State Unified Court System.

Social Security number (SSN)

Enter your nine-digit Social Security number (SSN).

Legal name of business:

Enter the legal name of your business exactly as it appears on your Certificate of Incorporation, Certificate of Authority, or other official document identifying your business.

Your preparer tax identification number (PTIN):

If you have one, enter your nine-character federal preparer tax identification number.

Your New York tax preparer registration ID number (NYTPRIN):

Enter your eight-digit New York tax preparer registration ID number (NYTPRIN). If you do not have an NYTPRIN, leave this field blank.
