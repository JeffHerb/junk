Instructions

Phone number

Enter a ten-digit phone number. International phone numbers are not accepted.

Username

Usernames are not case sensitive and must be between 8 and 60 characters long. Use only letters, numbers, and these special characters: .@_-.

Temporary password

Enter a temporary password that is between 8 and 20 characters. Include at least one number and three letters. Don't use the same character more than two times. The first time you login to your Online Services account you will be prompted to change your temporary password to a permanent one.

Password

Passwords are case sensitive and must be between 8 and 20 characters long. You must include at least one number and at least three letters. Do not repeat the same character more than two times in a row. Special characters are allowed.
