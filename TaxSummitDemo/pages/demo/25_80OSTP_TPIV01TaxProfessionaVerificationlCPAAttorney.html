Instructions

Tax year you filed a return

Select the tax year an income tax return was filed.

Taxable income reported on your return

Enter the taxable income reported on your New York State income tax return (IT-201 or IT-203) or amended income tax return (IT-201-X or IT-203-X) for the year selected. If your taxable income is negative, enter a minus sign (-) in front of the number (Ex. -2500). Do not enter any dollar signs, commas, or decimals.

See Individual verification for instructions on where to find this amount on your return.

If you have filed only Form IT-214, Real Property Tax Credit, or Form NYC-210, School Tax Credit, you must choose a different verification method.

Requested refund amount

Enter the refund amount requested on your New York State income tax return (IT-201 or IT-203) or amended income tax return (IT-201-X or IT-203-X) for the year selected. Do not enter any dollar signs, commas, or decimals.

See Individual verification for instructions on where to find this amount on your return.

Amount you owed

Enter the amount you reported owed on your New York State income tax return (IT-201 or IT-203) or amended income tax return (IT-201-X or IT-203-X) for the year selected. If you did not owe an amount, enter a single '0.' Do not enter any dollar signs, commas, or decimals.

See Individual verification for instructions on where to find this amount on your return.
