Welcome, John Smith!

Learn about the tasks you can perform with your account.

Add Clients:

You can add clients to your account to immediately perform services and view messages on their behalf. Before you begin, you will need the following:

  • your client's signed E-ZRep Form TR-2000, Tax Information Access and Transaction Authorization ,
  • your client's taxpayer identification number, and
  • one of the client verification options below. Options include:
    • a filed NYS tax return,
    • a bill (assessment),
    • a five-digit PIN (provided by the Tax Department), or
    • confirming your client has not yet filed a NYS tax return.

Add Employees:

You can add your employees to your Tax Professional account. You will be able to select each employee's level of access and assign clients to each employee. To add an employee, you will need to provide the employee's:

  • email address, and
  • phone number.
E-ZRep Form TR-2000

E-ZRep Form TR-2000, Tax Information Access and Transaction Authorization, is required and allows a tax professional to receive and access a client's tax information and perform services on their behalf.
