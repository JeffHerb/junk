      Client taxpayer ID number

      Enter the Social Security number (SSN), employer identification number (EIN), or New York State tax identification number, including any letters at the beginning or end.

      Are you adding a client only to file a power of attorney on their behalf?
      • If you choose Yes, you do not need an E-ZRep form and will only be able to access the Power of attorney (POA) application on your client's behalf.
      • If you choose No, you must have an E-ZRep form completed and signed by your client. You will have access to applications based on the permissions your client has granted you on the E-ZRep form, including the Power of attorney (POA) application.
