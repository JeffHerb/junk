If you had an overpayment of New York State income tax or the Metropolitan Commuter Transportation Mobility Tax, you may need to report it on your federal income tax in the following year. To find out if you need to report this information on a federal income tax return, see the instructions for that return, or contact the IRS or a tax preparer.
