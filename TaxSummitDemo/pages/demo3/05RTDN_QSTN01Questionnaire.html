Instructions

Selection notice

You can find the notice number in the upper right corner or lower left corner of your notice.

Document locator number

You can find the document locator number (DLN) in the upper right hand corner of your notice.

If it is longer than 12 digits, enter only the first 12.

Case number

The case number begins with an X and is followed by nine digits. It is located either in the upper right-hand corner of your notice or in the subject line of your letter.

Notice/form ID

The notice or form ID is in either the upper right-hand corner or the lower left-hand corner of your notice.

Notice date

Please enter the date shown on the notice we sent you. If there is no date on your notice, please use today’s date in this format: MM/DD/YYYY.
